Book: Hyperbolic geometry

Hyperbolic geometry is an introductory book to hyperbolic geometry at the graduate level. It is based on a course taught at TU Darmstadt in the winter 2019-2020. The book comprises 11 chapters, 45 figures, 80 exercises with hints and solutions. Download (PDF).
