Book: Hyperbolic geometry

Hyperbolic geometry is a textbook that I have been working on since 2019, to appear in 2022. It is an introductory course on hyperbolic geometry at the early graduate or advanced undergraduate level.

The book comprises 18 chapters and an appendix, 100+ figures, 100+ exercises with hints and solutions.
